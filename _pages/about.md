A finance professional with more than a decade of experience in financial planning & analysis, financial reporting, and financial data analytics for the Ministry of Finance. This website comprises my working paper, research, and projects. My analytics project could also be accessed in [subreddit](http://reddit.com). You can put a picture in, too. The code is already in, just name your picture `prof_pic.jpg` and put it in the `img/` folder.

Put your address / P.O. box / other info right below your picture. You can also disable any of these elements by editing `profile` property of the YAML header of your `_pages/about.md`. Edit `_bibliography/papers.bib` and Jekyll will render your [publications page](/al-folio/publications/) automatically.
